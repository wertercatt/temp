Sori: Take Robert to your room.

You guys grab some drinks first, but then yeah. You suddenly realize that you haven’t had an INTRODUCTION yet, so here we go!

Your name is SORI SELENE CIENDA, and your name reflects your general status as a bit of a MUTT. You’re not quite sure what everything is, but you can see ARABIC, GREEK, and BUTCHERED SPANISH in your name alone, and to be honest while your FATHER is pretty BRITISH (and Greek) you think your MOM had some HINDI in there somewhere too. It’s kind of a bizarre multiethnic mess running through your genes, but hey! The cultures aren’t surviving, but hopefully your genes might. However, your general outlook on life says that this totally isn’t going to happen. You’re pretty sure that everyone is doomed and going to DIE horribly. Humanity hasn’t gained any ground at any point during the war. Just lost it.

But whatever. You tell yourself you just feel like this because everything went so badly at AMBAM. When you were there, you cultivated a variety of interests, such as EXERCISING. You’re really big on finding ways to go on invigorating runs through the TUNNELS. You also used to indulge in a VARIETY OF PUZZLES. Jigsaws, Sudoku, Spot-the-Difference… It didn’t really matter. It was a great way to keep your head clear and sharp as a tack! As such, SCIENCE has always been cool to you. CHEMISTRY, PHYSICS, you don’t really care and to some extent you don’t really see the difference. One is a subset of the other but it’s all very cool, if somewhat depressing in its own right. You’ve always hoped someone might find a way to defy the SECOND LAW OF THERMODYNAMICS, but that probably isn’t happening. Oh well. You’ll be long DEAD before that matters anyway. S’why your CLOTHES are so sad. The black is obligations, but the SAD FACE is totally your thing. You own that shit, a permanent “MEMENTO MORI” written across your chest.

You have been a PILOT all your life more or less, and while it has put you in a PRIVILEGED POSITION, it’s also messed you up in a few ways as well. You’re not good at keeping FRIENDS, though you’re fine at making them. You just don’t have the TIME. And worse, because people have been picking up after you your whole life… You’re kind of a MESS. Oh god this is hugely embarrassing I mean, you and Robert got DRINKS and they’re probably two of these cups but which ones are FRESH and which are the ones you haven’t thrown out? And there’s BOOKS and FOOD TRAYS everywhere and you left your UNIFORM on the floor and gosh. Robert, who was already being kind of SHY all of a sudden, as if responding to a prompt without that being depicted onscreen, is kind of SPUTTERING while you dash around cleaning stuff up. Welp.

What on Earth are you going to do now?
